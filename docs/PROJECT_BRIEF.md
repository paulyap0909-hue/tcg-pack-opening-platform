# TCG Pack Opening Platform — Project Brief

## 1. Project Name

Working Name: Treasure Pack TCG

This is a futuristic online card pack opening platform inspired by Pokémon TCG and One Piece Card Game culture.

The goal is not to build a normal ecommerce website, but a gaming-style card pack opening experience where users can browse packs, open packs online, collect cards in their vault, join raffles, view leaderboards and request shipping.

---

## 2. Core Concept

A technology + gaming inspired TCG platform where users can:

* Browse Pokémon-style and One Piece-style card packs
* Open card packs online with animated reveal effects
* View chase cards and rarity pool
* Store pulled cards in their personal inventory
* Request shipment for physical cards
* Join limited drops and raffles
* Compete on leaderboard
* Invite friends and earn rewards

---

## 7. Core User Flow

User enters website
↓
User browses Pokémon / One Piece packs
↓
User views pack details and chase cards
↓
User tops up wallet / points
↓
User clicks Open Pack
↓
Animated pack reveal starts
↓
Card result is revealed with rarity effect
↓
Card enters user inventory
↓
User can continue opening or request shipping

---

## 8. Homepage Structure

The homepage should include:

1. Hero Section
   Headline: Open Your Next Chase Card Online
   Subheadline: Pokémon & One Piece inspired card packs, mystery drops and collector pulls. Open instantly, collect digitally and ship anytime.
   CTA: Start Opening / View Packs

2. Featured Packs
   Show Pokémon Packs and One Piece Packs.

3. Top Chase Cards
   Show high-value chase cards.

4. How It Works
   Top Up → Open Pack → Reveal Card → Store or Ship

5. Live Pulls
   Show recent cards opened by users.

6. Leaderboard Preview
   Show top openers / top collectors.

7. Community Section
   Invite users to join WhatsApp / Discord / Telegram community.

## 9. Pack Lobby Requirements

Each pack card should show:

* Pack name
* Pack category: Pokémon / One Piece
* Price in points
* Remaining quantity
* Top chase card
* Rarity level
* Open Now button
* View Details button

The lobby should feel like a game store, not a normal product grid.

---

## 10. Pack Detail Requirements

Each pack detail page should show:

* Pack cover
* Price
* Total packs
* Remaining packs
* Chase card list
* Card pool preview
* Rarity breakdown
* Guaranteed floor value
* Terms
* Open Pack button

The page should feel transparent and trustworthy.

## 14. Important UI Rules

Do:

* Use dark futuristic background
* Use neon glow
* Use holographic cards
* Use animated buttons
* Use pack opening animation
* Use mobile-first layout
* Use game-like microinteractions

Do not:

* Do not make it look like normal ecommerce
* Do not copy official Pokémon or One Piece assets illegally
* Do not claim official affiliation
* Do not use copyrighted logos without permission
* Do not make random pack odds unclear
* Do not hide terms from users
